Curriculum & Learning
What is Agentic AI and why is it important?
Agentic AI refers to AI systems that can act autonomously — setting goals, making decisions, and taking actions without constant human direction.

It's one of the most in-demand AI skills globally. AI Champ's Agentic AI track teaches you how to design, build, and deploy AI agents that can automate tasks, conduct research, write code, and much more — skills that will define the 2030 job market.
What is Promptly Pro?
Promptly Pro is AI Champ's flagship Prompt Engineering workshop — the art and science of communicating effectively with AI systems to get the best results.

In this module, you learn advanced techniques including chain-of-thought prompting, role-setting, few-shot learning, and structured output formatting — skills that are immediately applicable across all AI tools and career contexts.
Do I need coding knowledge to join?
No coding knowledge is required to join.

For the Students workshop, Python programming is introduced from scratch as part of the curriculum (Module M5).

For the Professionals programme, the curriculum is designed to be accessible without a technical background. Optional advanced coding modules are available for those interested.
What AI tools will participants work with?
Participants will get hands-on experience with the most widely used AI tools:

🤖 ChatGPT (OpenAI) · Gemini (Google) · Claude (Anthropic)
💻 Microsoft Copilot · Perplexity AI · Midjourney / DALL-E
🛠️ Python AI libraries · Custom AI Agent frameworks
📊 AI-powered productivity tools for workplace automation
